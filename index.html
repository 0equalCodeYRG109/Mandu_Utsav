Introduction to Mandu Festival

Mandu Festival is a vibrant celebration of music, dance, and culture, held annually in the historic city of Mandu, Madhya Pradesh. The festival showcases the rich cultural heritage of the region, featuring traditional music and dance performances, handicraft exhibitions, and delicious local cuisine.

Festival Highlights

  • Music Performances
  • Dance Shows
  • Food Festivals
  • Handicraft Exhibitions
